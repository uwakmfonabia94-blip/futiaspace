About FutiaSpace

Updated: April 2026

FutiaSpace is the student directory built exclusively for Federal University of Technology, Ikot Abasi (FUTIA) students. It exists for one reason — to help students find, discover, and connect with their coursemates.

Before FutiaSpace, knowing who was in your department and level meant physically showing up to class or asking around. FutiaSpace changes that. You can see every student in your department, your level, their face, their name, and a little about who they are.

Who Built This

FutiaSpace is built by Uwakmfon Theophilus, a Computer Science student at FUTIA. It is an independent student project — not affiliated with or endorsed by the university administration.

What's Coming

Version 1 gives you the directory and the poke. Version 2 will bring a social feed, departmental group chats, and direct messaging. Updates arrive automatically through the app — you'll be notified when something new lands.

Privacy Policy

Updated: April 2026

What We Collect

When you create a FutiaSpace account, we collect your full name, email address, password, department, level, gender, optional bio, and profile photo. We also record your last activity time to surface recently active students in the directory.

How We Use It

Your information is used solely to display your profile in the student directory and to power core features like poke notifications. We do not use your data for advertising, third-party profiling, or any purpose beyond what the app provides.

Who Can See Your Profile

Your profile — name, photo, department, level, gender, and bio — is visible to any verified FutiaSpace student with an account. Your email address and password are never visible to other students under any circumstances.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. Supabase complies with industry-standard security practices and data protection regulations.

Your Rights

You can update your profile information at any time through the Edit Profile page. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact us using the Report a Problem option in the app menu.

Contact

For privacy concerns, use the Report a Problem option in the menu to reach us directly on WhatsApp.

FAQ

Updated: April 2026

What is FutiaSpace?

FutiaSpace is a student directory for FUTIA students. Browse your coursemates by department and level, and poke anyone to start a connection. More features — posts, chats, and more — are coming in future versions.

Who can see my profile?

Any verified FutiaSpace student with an account can see your profile. The directory is not publicly accessible on the internet — only signed-up students can view it.

What is a Poke?

A Poke is how you say "hey" to a coursemate. When you poke someone, they get a notification. If they poke back, you've made a connection. Direct messaging is coming in a future update.

Can I poke someone more than once?

No. You can only poke someone once until they poke you back. This keeps things intentional and respectful.

How do I change my department or level?

Go to your profile, tap Edit, then update your department or level. Changing your department will move you to a different section of the directory.

Can I install FutiaSpace on my phone?

Yes. Open FutiaSpace in your browser and tap the Install prompt at the bottom of the screen. It will be added to your home screen like a regular app — no app store needed.

Why is my department directory empty?

FutiaSpace is still growing. If no one from your department and level has joined yet, the directory will be empty for that filter. Share your invite link with coursemates to grow it faster.

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email. A reset link will be sent to your inbox. Follow the link to set a new password.

How do I report a student?

Use the Report a Problem option in the app menu to send us a message on WhatsApp. Describe the issue and include the student's name.

Community Guidelines

Updated: April 2026

FutiaSpace is for FUTIA students. To keep it safe and respectful for everyone, every student must follow these guidelines.

  1. Use Your Real Identity

    Sign up with your real name and a clear profile photo. FutiaSpace is built on trust between actual FUTIA students. Fake or misleading accounts will be removed.

  2. Be Respectful

    Treat every student the way you'd want to be treated. Harassment, insults, and targeted behaviour have no place here.

  3. Poke Responsibly

    Pokes are for genuine connection. Using the poke feature to annoy, harass, or spam another student is a violation of these guidelines.

  4. No Impersonation

    Do not create an account pretending to be another student, a lecturer, or any university official.

  5. Protect Others' Privacy

    Do not screenshot or share other students' profiles or personal information outside FutiaSpace without their consent.

  6. Report Problems

    If you see something that violates these guidelines, report it using the Report a Problem option in the menu. Do not take matters into your own hands.

Violation of these guidelines may result in your account being suspended or permanently removed from FutiaSpace.
